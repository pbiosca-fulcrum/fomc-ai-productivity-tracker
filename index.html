Michael Barr — Three Scenarios (Feb 17 '26)  Source

Scenario 1 (Gradual): AI = normal GPT like electricity. Manageable displacement. "Most consistent with current evidence" per Barr.
Scenario 2 (Rapid): Mass professional job displacement. Society rethinks safety nets.
Scenario 3 (Stall): Plateau from data/energy constraints. >$1T in wasted debt.

Barr cited: 17% all firms using AI, 75% large, <10% small (Census BTOS survey).
